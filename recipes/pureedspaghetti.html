Pureed spaghetti

Description

Crowds' favorite dessert, with the perfect balance of sweet to saucy, tender to raw, and memorable to disgusting.

Ingredients

  • Leftover spaghetti
  • Snow sugar

Directions

  1. Just get your leftover spaghetti out of the fridge (if it was in there).
  2. Put it in the blender or processor until it gets the desired puree texture.
  3. Top the pureed spaghetti with snow sugar, serve yourself and enjoy!
