Doritos cereal

Description

A mouth-watering appetizer for that long span between dinner and breakfast.

Ingredients

  • One bag of spicy Doritos
  • One cup of warm milk

Directions

  1. Just gently open the bag and empty the content inside a bowl.
  2. Pour the warm milk over the bowl of Doritos (milk can be cold instead).
  3. The process can be done the other way around, as you prefer.
