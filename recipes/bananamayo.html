Banana mayo sandwich

Description

A banana mayo sandwich made in the middle of the night is so simple and quick that it feels like cheating, but it's not: This is the real stuff.

Ingredients

  • Sandwich bread, as much as you desire
  • Mayonnaise
  • Banana(s)

Directions

  1. Spread abundant mayo over your preferred type of sandwich bread (or the one you have at hand).
  2. Chop your banana(s) into thin slices, can also be thick.
  3. Assemble your banana mayo sandwich, and cut it in half.
